ELON MUSK Elon Musk is a visionary entrepreneur and business magnate known for his pioneering work in multiple industries. Born in 1971 in South Africa, Musk co-founded Zip2, which became his first successful venture. He later co-founded X.com, which evolved into PayPal and was sold to eBay. Musk's greatest impact, however, came through his role in founding and leading SpaceX, Tesla, Inc., and SolarCity. His work with SpaceX revolutionized the space industry with ambitious goals to make space travel more accessible. As the CEO of Tesla, he spearheaded the electric vehicle revolution and sustainable energy solutions. Musk's relentless drive and innovative thinking have made him a prominent figure in the tech and space industries, earning him recognition as one of the most influential and high-profile figures in the world.
